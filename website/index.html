Your terminal.

Check every workspace, catch agent handoffs, and send the answer without reopening your laptop. vmux Remote connects directly to your relay over Tailscale or your local network.

From zero to attached

One command.
Your workspace survives.

  1. 01

    Install

    cargo install vmux-tui

    Or use the checksum-verified prebuilt binary for Linux and macOS.

  2. 02

    Attach

    vmux attach

    The daemon starts automatically and keeps every pane alive.

  3. 03

    Connect your agents

    vmux hooks install

    Agent status appears in vmux without changing your workflow.

Local by architecture

Your terminal data stays on your machines.

The CLI and relay talk to your local vmux daemon. Remote access crosses your own tailnet—not ours.
